Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number the start of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking the crystal you will aad a specific ammount of points to your total score. You will win the game by matching the total score to the random number, you will lose the game if your total score goes above the random number. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts the game will change the value of each crystal.
